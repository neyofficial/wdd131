Homowo Festival

The Homowo Festival, celebrated by the Ga people of Ghana, marks a time of victory over famine and is full of joyous celebration.

Aboakyir Festival

The Aboakyir Festival is a hunting festival celebrated by the people of Winneba to honor their deity with a live catch of a wild animal.
