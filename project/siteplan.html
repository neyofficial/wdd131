Ghanaian Cultural Heritage - Website Site Plan

Connect to your African roots in Ghana

Site Name

Discover Ghanaian Heritage

This name was selected because it reflects the website's focus on exploring and sharing key elements of Ghana's rich cultural traditions. The term "Discover" suggests learning and exploration, inviting visitors to learn more about the country’s heritage.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to create awareness and appreciation for Ghanaian cultural heritage. It provides visitors with an in-depth understanding of key Ghanaian festivals, traditional foods, and important landmarks. The site will serve as a valuable resource for anyone interested in learning more about Ghana, including tourists, cultural enthusiasts, and people of Ghanaian descent.

Scenarios

  • What are the most popular festivals in Ghana, and when are they celebrated?
  • How is the Chale Wote Street Art Festival celebrated in Accra?
  • How can I learn to cook popular Ghanaian dishes like Jollof rice and Banku?

Color Schema

The color schema for this website will be inspired by the vibrant colors seen in traditional Ghanaian kente cloth:

  • Primary Color (Header, Footers): #E8A317 (Golden Yellow) – to reflect the bright, warm colors of Ghanaian culture.
  • Secondary Color (Background, Button): #2C3539 (Charcoal Gray) – to contrast with the primary color and add elegance.
  • Accent Color (Links, Highlights): #3BB9FF (Sky Blue) – for emphasis and to add a cool balance.

Typography

  • Headings (h1, h2, etc.): "Poppins", sans-serif – A modern, clean font for clear emphasis and readability.
  • Body Text: "Roboto", sans-serif – A highly readable and neutral font to ensure the text is easily understood.
