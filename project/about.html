History

NEY Rafting Company was founded in 2010 with the mission to provide unforgettable rafting experiences. Over the years, we have grown to become one of the most trusted names in adventure rafting, known for our safety and customer satisfaction. Our company started with just one raft and a dream. Today, we operate multiple rafting locations, offering a variety of adventure levels to suit all types of thrill-seekers. Join us as we continue to make history one rapid at a time.

Adventure Awaits You!
